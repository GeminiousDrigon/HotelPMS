Blue Pool Garden Hotel

Bluepool Garden was built for vacation purposes only after the earthquake last 2013. The proprietor Maria Paz Bacareza and the financer Edita Amplayo started planning and building Bluepool Garden last February 20, 2017, in Bood, Maribojoc, Bohol. As the project was going on, they have both decided to make it as a business resort, instead of making it exclusive for their relatives only.

November 30, 2017, was the official opening of the Bluepool Garden. The proprietor Maria Paz Bacareza manages it. The resort contains two swimming pools, for adults and kids. It also has a comfortable cottage and rooms to stay in.

The Bluepool Garden celebrated its first anniversary last November 30, 2018. In the first year of the business, they held already several occasions like birthdays and weddings and many more. Now, the business grows bigger and bigger as they started to build a multi-purpose function Hall for the occasion.
